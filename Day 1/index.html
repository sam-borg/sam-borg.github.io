How to make a PBJ

  1. Obtain your favorite Penut butter, Jelly, and butter knife
  2. Open containers
  3. spread penut butter on one piece of bread, and jelly on the other piece of bread
  4. press both side that have the penut butter with the jelly together
  5. slice diagonally with a knife
  6. Enjoy!
